What is Kids Hope


Kids Hope is an organization partnered with a local church to provide structure, training, and support in public schools to give students what they need most – hope and love from a mentor


Local churches provide mentors who go through a thorough screening process, background check, and training to begin a one-on-one mentoring relationship once a week with a student.


What is Kids Hope in Medina, Ohio


We are currently working with only 30% of the elementary schools in Medina providing mentors to 29 students who need them.  Our long-term goal is to provide mentors to all 7 schools and help provide hope and love to all the students that could benefit from a mentor.

How You Can Help


We have an ongoing need for mentors to come along side a student and let them know that someone has a personal relationship with them; that they are loved and that they matter.  If you attend Heartland Community Church at either campus, please consider becoming a mentor.  Your first step is to fill out the form below and a local member of Kids Hope will contact you to discuss more in detail what is required and how you can help.
